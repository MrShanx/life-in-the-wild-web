Life in the Wild : Fun Facts

Horses can sleep both lying down and standing up.
Only one mammal can’t jump — the elephant.
Koi fish symbolize persistence, determination, wealth, success, and good fortune
Crocodiles are the closest living relatives of birds.
The name Lemur is actually Latin for “spirits of the night”.
To attract a female partner, male capuchin monkeys will urinate in their hands and then rub it thoroughly into their fur.
